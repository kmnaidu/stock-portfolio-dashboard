## POST-LAUNCH PLAYBOOK

### First 4 Hours (Critical)
- Stay online and reply to every comment within 5-15 minutes
- LinkedIn algorithm rewards engagement velocity → faster replies = more reach
- Even simple "Thank you 🙏" replies count

### First 24 Hours
- DM 10-15 close contacts personally asking them to repost
- Engage with senior people's recent posts (visibility boost)
- Reply to all DMs within a few hours

### Week 1
- Track which roles/companies appear in comments and DMs
- Follow up with everyone who showed interest (even casual mentions)
- Post a thank-you update at end of week showing momentum

### Tactical Tips
1. **Pin this post** in your Featured section after publishing
2. **Don't edit the post** in first 24 hours (LinkedIn de-prioritizes edited posts)
3. **Reply with details** if someone asks about specific tech (shows depth)
4. **Avoid arguing** in comments — keep it positive and professional

## EMOTIONAL READINESS CHECK

Before posting, ask yourself:
- ✅ Am I ready for friends/family to know?
- ✅ Am I ready for some awkward conversations?
- ✅ Am I ready to respond to 50+ DMs in next 48 hours?
- ✅ Am I OK with this being visible on my activity history forever?

If yes to all → post it.
If hesitant → wait 24 hours and reconsider.

Either way, the post is ready when you are.
